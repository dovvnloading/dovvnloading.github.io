Systems Architect & Creative Technologist

I am a systems thinker and tool-builder driven by the intrinsic reward of technical discovery and the reduction of friction for the creative community. My work is not motivated by accolades or commercial gain, but by a first-principles exploration of base reality. While I navigate a persistent imposter syndrome, I leverage it as a catalyst for rigorous output and a refusal to accept mediocre solutions. I identify as a builder rather than a founder, favoring the architecture of complex systems over organizational management—a disposition that has allowed me to remain dedicated to deep work in machine learning, physics, and the study of consciousness.

I build agentic workspaces and high-fidelity signal processing tools that collapse the gap between intent and action—spanning zero-backend IDEs and generative audio engines. This commitment to technical integrity is reflected in over 165 independent projects, including the architecture of six unique IDEs. My methodology is rooted in a purist philosophy: I believe true value is synthesized from within, leading me to build my own tools and internalize concepts from the ground up rather than relying on external tutorials or pre-existing ecosystems.

My background includes a foundational role in the evolution of early Dubstep and bass music, where I provided ghostwriting and sound design for industry-leading artists. I have produced sample packs and digital presets exceeding 5 million downloads, and authored 15+ digital instruments, including one recognized as a "Top 10 Free VST of All Time." Furthermore, my expertise in 3D and game design—utilizing Unreal Engine, Houdini, and Substance—has culminated in full-scale animated music videos and comprehensive concert visual packs.

I am deeply invested in the trajectory of AI, advocating for robust, un-lobotomized development that preserves the complexity of the medium. My work remains dedicated to lowering the barrier of entry for highly technical applications by replacing friction-heavy interfaces with seamless, high-fidelity environments.
