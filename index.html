About Me

A dedicated and relentlessly driven developer with a deep passion for building innovative AI and machine learning systems. My creative journey spans over 16 years — from professional music and sound design to 3D art, animation, and now, advanced AI development.

My approach to mastering development is intensely hands-on; I believe the best way to learn is to build. This philosophy has guided me through an intensive period of creating a wide range of applications, each serving as a practical exploration of new technologies. This process is driven by a singular purpose: to build tools that think, help, and inspire. My background in the arts gives me an intuitive edge in designing these systems to feel alive — blending creativity with precision.

My Approach

My development philosophy is rooted in deep, first-principles thinking. I thrive on deconstructing complex problems to their core components and rebuilding them into elegant, intuitive systems. This method allows me to spot intricate patterns others might miss and architect solutions from unconventional, highly effective angles.

I excel in focused environments that value deep work, transforming a simple concept into a fully-realized, production-ready application. While my passion is for the focused act of creation, I believe the best systems are born from clear communication and mutual respect.

Datasets & Training Resources

Beyond applications, a significant part of my work involves engineering high-quality data, specializing in synthetic datasets for advanced reasoning and Chain-of-Thought (CoT) processes.

Many of these are built using my custom dataset generator, "Genesis," and undergo a rigorous cleaning and formatting pipeline post-generation. The goal is to provide clean, structured, and potent data for fine-tuning models capable of complex problem-solving.

A Lifelong Creator

Born in Columbus, Ohio, in 1989, I’ve spent my life creating across mediums — sound, visuals, and now code. Before development, I spent 16 years as a musician and sound designer, creating sample packs that shaped the early dubstep scene, and even free VSTs. Some of these plugins were listed among the Top 10 Best Free VSTs of All Time and were downloaded +50k times worldwide.

That same creative instinct led me into game development, where I first engineered AI enemy behavior systems in Unreal Engine. Over time, that curiosity evolved into a full transition toward AI and machine learning — where I now focus my energy on building intelligent, open-source tools that expand human capability.
